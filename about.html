About D&D Hub

This website is dedicated to my friends Jake, Ben & David/Tristan. Thank you for allowing me to be your game master for Waterdeep: Dungeon of The Mad Mage. Without gamers, there is no game.

We all live seperate lives, but distance and time does not stop us from meeting atleast once a week(most of the time) to delve into the ultimate mega-dungeon, Undermountain. With this said, I value my time(and yours) very much and am truly honoured to say that I am still met with the same enthusiasm each session as when we had first started years ago.

One of the goals for this website are to act as a centralized location or time capsule, containing some of the content from our D&D campaign such as imagery, NPCs, quests, significant narrative events, character sheets and more. I hope that i can share some insight on how it went, how its going and what to look forward to for our game. This website is a work in progress so please dont mind the scaffolding.

- Greg Rivera | GM | Web Designer
